Job Description: Chef Cuisinier

Restaurant: Le Jardin Gastronomique
Location: Paris, France

About the Restaurant:
Le Jardin Gastronomique is a Michelin-starred fine dining restaurant celebrating innovative French cuisine. We are seeking an exceptional Chef Cuisinier to lead our culinary team and continue our tradition of gastronomic excellence.

Job Responsibilities:
1. Culinary Leadership
- Develop and create innovative menu concepts
- Maintain the highest standards of culinary excellence
- Oversee kitchen operations and staff management
- Ensure consistent food quality and presentation
- Create seasonal tasting menus
- Develop signature dishes that reflect the restaurant's vision

2. Kitchen Management
- Manage kitchen staff of 15-20 culinary professionals
- Develop and train kitchen team
- Implement and maintain strict hygiene and safety standards
- Control food costs and inventory management
- Design staff schedules and workflow
- Coordinate with sous chefs and kitchen departments

3. Menu and Ingredient Expertise
- Source high-quality, local, and seasonal ingredients
- Develop relationships with local farmers and suppliers
- Create innovative fusion of traditional French techniques with modern culinary trends
- Manage menu pricing and food cost margins
- Develop wine and food pairing recommendations

Technical Requirements:
- Minimum 7-10 years of high-end restaurant experience
- Advanced French culinary techniques
- Proven track record in Michelin-starred restaurants
- Expert knowledge of French and international cuisines
- Strong understanding of food presentation and plating
- Advanced kitchen management skills

Required Skills:
- Extensive knowledge of French culinary traditions
- Expertise in classical and modern cooking techniques
- Advanced pastry and sauce preparation skills
- Menu development and recipe creation
- Kitchen equipment and technology proficiency
- Cost control and inventory management

Professional Qualifications:
- Culinary Arts Degree from a recognized institution
- Advanced culinary certifications
- Experience in fine dining and haute cuisine
- Proven leadership in professional kitchen environments

Soft Skills:
- Creative culinary vision
- Exceptional leadership abilities
- Stress management in high-pressure environments
- Team motivation and development
- Attention to detail
- Innovative thinking

Additional Benefits:
