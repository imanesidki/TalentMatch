Location: [Specify Location] / Remote / Hybrid

Job Summary
We are seeking a detail-oriented and experienced Accountant to manage financial records, ensure compliance with tax regulations, and oversee financial transactions. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills, knowledge of accounting principles, and proficiency in financial software.

Key Responsibilities
Maintain accurate financial records, including ledgers, invoices, and bank statements.

Prepare financial reports, balance sheets, and profit & loss statements.

Ensure compliance with tax laws and assist in tax filing and audits.

Manage accounts payable and receivable, ensuring timely payments and collections.

Conduct reconciliations of bank statements and financial data.

Assist in budget preparation and financial forecasting.

Work with internal teams to improve financial efficiency and cost management.

Use accounting software such as QuickBooks, SAP, or Xero to manage transactions.

Required Skills & Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.

2+ years of experience in accounting or financial management.

Proficiency in accounting software (QuickBooks, SAP, Xero, etc.).

Strong knowledge of tax regulations, financial reporting, and audits.

Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.

Attention to detail and high level of accuracy.

Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

Preferred Qualifications
Experience in corporate finance, payroll management, or cost accounting.

Knowledge of financial regulations and IFRS/GAAP standards.

Ability to work independently and meet strict deadlines.
